How to learn

The Pro Max philosophy

Four principles that separate engineers who crack FAANG from those who just watch videos.

Use your own logic
Never copy variables or logic. Understand the concept, then build your own approach from scratch.
Extract core concepts
Zoom out from the problem. Identify the pattern and implement the idea in your own way.
Understand the "why"
Analyze why each part of the solution exists. Deeper understanding beats memorizing code.
Prepare for advanced
A solid foundation here means seamless adaptation to harder problems at the next level.
