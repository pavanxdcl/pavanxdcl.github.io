// 04 — FAQ
Common Questions
Do I really need to finish the Rookie Batch first?
Yes. The Pro Batch builds directly on Rookie foundations. Skipping ahead will make the content significantly harder to absorb and you'll miss key intuition building blocks.
How does the 1:1 mentorship actually work?
Once enrolled, you schedule sessions directly with Pavan Sir. Sessions are conducted live via video call where you can share your screen, get code reviewed, and discuss your progress. You also get direct WhatsApp access between sessions.
Is the batch fully live or are there recorded sessions too?
The core content includes both live sessions and curated materials. The 1:1 mentorship is always live. Contact Pavan Sir for the current schedule format.
How many seats are available in the Pro Batch?
Seats are intentionally limited to maintain the quality of 1:1 mentorship. Reach out early to secure your spot — cohorts fill up fast.
What companies can I target after completing the Pro Batch?
The curriculum is designed with product company interviews in mind — FAANG, unicorn startups, and top Indian tech firms. Pavan Sir also provides company-specific prep guidance as part of placement support.
